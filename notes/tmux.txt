- Start: `tmux`
- Session:
  - Detach: `Ctrl-b d`
  - Reattach: `tmux attach`
    - `tmux list-sessions`
    - `tmux attach -t <session-name>`
- Window:
  - New window: `Ctrl-b c`
  - Switch window:
    - Next: `Ctrl-b n`
    - Previous: `Ctrl-b p`
    - Specific: `Ctrl-b <number>`
- Pane:
  - Split screen vertically: `Ctrl-b %`
  - Split screen horizontally: `Ctrl-b "`
  - Switch between panes: `Ctrl-b o`
- Kill:
  - Pane: `Ctrl-b x`
  - Window: `Ctrl-b &`
  - Session: `exit` from all windows or `tmux kill-session -t <name>`
